The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Lord of the Rings

The Lord of the Rings is a fantasy novel by J.R.R. Tolkien that follows hobbit Frodo Baggins on his quest to destroy the One Ring, a powerful artifact created by the dark lord Sauron. Along with his companions, including other hobbits, a wizard, a dwarf, an elf, and a human, Frodo journeys through Middle-earth, facing various dangers and battles. Ultimately, Frodo and his loyal friend Sam reach Mount Doom, where the Ring must be destroyed, and with the help of unexpected allies, they succeed in saving Middle-earth from the tyranny of Sauron.

Back to the Future

Back to the Future is a science fiction movie about a high school student named Marty McFly, who accidentally travels back in time to 1955 in a time-traveling car built by his eccentric scientist friend, Doc Brown. In the past, he meets his parents and interferes with their meeting, putting his own existence at risk. With the help of the younger Doc Brown, Marty must find a way to reunite his parents and get back to his own time before he's trapped in the past forever.

Mr. Deeds

Mr. Deeds is a comedy movie about a small-town guy named Longfellow Deeds who inherits a vast fortune and moves to the big city, where he is quickly taken advantage of by greedy opportunists. Despite their attempts to exploit him, Deeds remains true to himself and falls in love with a reporter who initially tricked him for a story. In the end, Deeds exposes the fraudsters and wins the love of the reporter, proving that money can't buy happiness.
